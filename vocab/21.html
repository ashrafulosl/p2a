1. A 'pilgrim' is a person who undertakes a journey to a-

a. Mosque
b. A new country
c. Holy place
d. Bazar

Explanation:

Answer: Pilgrim .

Pilgrim অর্থ তীর্থযাত্রী, যারা সাধারণত কোনো পবিত্র স্থানের উদ্দেশ্যে

যাত্রা করে।

2. The people who carry a coffin at a funeral are called-

a. undertakers
b. supporters
c. pallbearers
d. mourners

Explanation:

Answer: pallbearers.

যারা coffin বা শবাধার বহন (carry) করে তাদেরকে বলা হয় pallbearer।

3. A person unable to pay his debts-

a. solvent
b. miser
c. poor
d. insolvent

Explanation:

Answer: insolvent.

ঋণ পরিশোধে অক্ষম ব্যক্তিকে বলা হয় insolvent (ঋণ পরিশোধে অসমর্থ দেউলিয়া)।

4. A person who knows many language is called-

a. multi-lingual
b. bilingual
c. polyglot
d. monolingual

Explanation:

Answer: ployglot.

যে ব্যক্তি অনেক ভাষা জানেন তাকে এককথায় বলে polyglot (বহুভাষিক)।

5. A person who is very interested in and enthusiastic about someone or something.

a. A. Sceptic
b. B. Ascetic
c. C. Devotee
d. D. Antiquarian

Explanation: Answer: Devotee. (উপাসক)

6. A person who loves everybody:

a. A. Cosmopolitan
b. B. Fratricide
c. C. Altruistic
d. D. Aristocrat

Explanation:

Answer: C. Altruistic.

এমন কেউ যে দাতব্য দান করে মানুষের মঙ্গল বাড়ানোর উদ্দেশ্যে। পরোপকারী পরহিতৈষী, পরোপকারী.

7. A tendency to see the worst aspect of things or believe that the worst will happen.

a. A. Fatalist
b. B. Optimist
c. C. Pessimist
d. D. Parsimonious

Explanation:

Answer: C. Pessimist.

হতাশাবাদী,মন্দগ্রাহী ব্যক্তি,দুঃখবাদী.

8. One who cannot easily pleased:

a. A. Cosmopolitan
b. B. Frightening
c. C. Fastidious
d. D. Feminist

Explanation:

Answer: C. Fastidious.

খুতখুতে স্বভাবের যে।

9. A formal expression of disapproval.

a. A. Criticise
b. B. Rebuff
c. C. Reprimand
d. D. Rebut

Explanation:

Answer: C. Reprimand.

মানে তিরস্কার করা।

10. A disease which spreads by contact:

a. A.Incurable
b. B.Infectious
c. C.Contagious
d. D.Fatal

Explanation:

Answer: C.Contagious.

এর অর্থ সংক্রামক।

Incurable=অনিরা্য়মযোগ্য.

Fatal=প্রাণঘাতি.

11. A man having exceptional talent or natural ability.

a. A. Versatile
b. B. Nubile
c. C. Exceptional
d. D. Gifted

Explanation:

Answer: Gifted.

এর অর্থ বিশেষ উপহার প্রাপ্ত।

12. Extreme or irrational fear of confined places

a. A. Schizophrenia
b. B. Claustrophobia
c. C. Kleptomania
d. D. Megalomania

Explanation: Answer: B. Claustrophobia.

13. A person who knows many foreign languages:

a. A. Linguist
b. B. Grammarian
c. C. Polyglot
d. D. Bilingual

Explanation:

Answer: A. Linguist.

ভাষাবিদ.

14. Habitually silent or talking little:

a. A. Servile
b. B. Unequivocal
c. C. Taciturn
d. D. Synoptic

Explanation:

Answer: Taciturn. (স্বল্পভাষী)

Syno: Reticent, Silent.

Anto: Verbose, Loquacious.

15. Antonym of the word 'lugubrious'-

a. Cheerful
b. mournful
c. manifest
d. malice

Explanation:

Answer: Cheerful.

Synonym of Lugubrious: Depressed, Dusky.

Antonym of Lugubrious: Jovial, Delightful.

16. He decided to turn his hobby into a lucrative sideline. Here 'lucrative' means-

a. unprofitable
b. profitable
c. absurd
d. controversial

Explanation:

Answer: profitable.

Syno: Beneficial, Advantageous, Remunerative.

Anto: Unprofitable.

17. Find out the odd word from the options-

a. perceptive
b. insightful
c. discerning
d. cunning

Explanation:

Answer: cunning (ধুর্ত)

perceptive- সহজে প্রত্যক্ষ করে এমন

discerning-নির্ণায়ক

insighful-অন্তর্দৃষ্টিপূর্ণ

18. Find out the odd word from the options-

a. willful
b. intentional
c. obvious
d. calculated

Explanation: Answer: obvious.

20. What would be the best antonym of "hibernate"?

a. dormancy
b. liveliness
c. sluggishness
d. democracy

Explanation:

Hibernate=remain inactive or indoors for an extended period.

21. Religion : Ritual :: Society : ?

a. Obligation
b. Law
c. Custom
d. Tradition

Answer: Custom.

Religion এ Rituals থাকে। Society এ Custom (আচার) থাকে।
